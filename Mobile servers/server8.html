Sir Winston Leonard Spencer Churchill was a British statesman, soldier, and writer who served as Prime Minister of the United Kingdom twice, from 1940 to 1945 during the Second World War, and again from 1951 to 1955.

He Winston Churchill was born in November 30, 1874 in the Blenheim Palace, United Kingdom. To sadly to pass away in January 24, 1965, Kensington, London, United Kingdom.

Winston grew up with social status, privilege, and a keen sense of heritage, but little money. In the custom of the day, he was raised by a nanny and sent to boarding schools. It was a lonely and hard life for a sensitive and imaginative child.

Blenheim Palace was always one of Churchill's favourite places. He spent much of his time as a child there, both before he went to school and during school holidays.
